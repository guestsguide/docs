As soon as you register a new guide, whether via a new account or your existing account, it will automatically be assigned to the 'Free Package'. This package is best suited for putting Guests Guide through its paces and trying out new things. 

Get to know all the services without obligation and then decide which non-free package suits you best.

:::danger[Attention]
The free package is not intended for live operation and is not publicly accessible
:::

## Services

- 5 pages
- 2 languages
- Logo & style of the house
- Smiley module
- Weather overview
- External links
- Guide is not publicly accessible!
